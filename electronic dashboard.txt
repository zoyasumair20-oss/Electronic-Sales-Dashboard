# ABC Electronics Sales Report


### Project Title

ABC Electronics Sales Report

### Objective

The objective of this project was to analyze a company's sales data using Microsoft Excel. Various Excel functions, Pivot Tables, charts, and data analysis techniques were used to generate meaningful business insights.

---

## Dataset

* Total Records: 50
* Company: ABC Electronics
* Columns:

  * Invoice ID
  * Date
  * Customer
  * Product
  * Category
  * Quantity
  * Unit Price
  * Salesperson
  * City

---

## Tasks Completed

### 1. Created Total Sales Column

Calculated Total Sales using the formula:

Quantity × Unit Price

Formula:
=F2*G2

---

### 2. Applied Discount

A 10% discount was applied to orders where Total Sales were greater than 100,000.

Formula:
=IF(J2>100000,J2*10%,0)

---

### 3. Calculated Net Sales

Formula:
=J2-K2

---

### 4. Calculated GST (18%)

Formula:
=L2*18%

---

### 5. Calculated Final Amount

Formula:
=L2+M2

---

## Data Analysis Performed

* Sorted data from Highest to Lowest Sales.
* Filtered records by Product and City.
* Applied Conditional Formatting to highlight high-value sales.
* Verified calculations using Excel formulas.
